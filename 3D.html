Chimera

For this project I was inspired by many different science fiction stories, the idea of mixing species to create a sort of chimera, and the idea of forcing skeletons from different species into an animal. For this, I took the skull of a deer and the skeleton on a Wooley Mammoth and mashed them into to body of a dog. Though this animal has the spine of a wooly mammoth sticking out of its back, as well as it's tusks out of its neck, and it seems to be wearing the skull of a deer over its head, I imagined it being alive. A sort of representation of what could go wrong in a science experiment.
