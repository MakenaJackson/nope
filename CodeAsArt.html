Soft Spoken

For this project I wanted to do something that looked a little softer and cleaner. I wanted to keep it interactive but use softer colors and shaped. I got rid of any sharp lines and used more muted colors. I wanted movement in the piece even without interaction, so I used what we learned in some of our in class assignments and created two objects that move across the page. In addition to this, there is a square that follows your curser throughout the piece, which once clicked will turn into a circle and four other circles will appear throughout the piece.
