Train Station

This project was in part inspired by Keiichi Matsuda's "HYPER-REALITY". I used After Effects as well as Illustrator and Photoshop in order to achieve my final result. Originally I had only planned on including the three images that disappear behind the train, but I felt that didn't quite fill the video nor did it make enough edits for me to consider the final product a piece I had created. So, I began to fill the piece with more of my own work, adding a piece I made previously in this class as well as a graphic I had made. I had planned on adding an animation of mine in, however, I was unable to import a GIF into After Effects, so I instead went with a graphic.
