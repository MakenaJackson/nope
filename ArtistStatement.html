Artist Statement

Makena Jackson

Though I have been working on art for most of my life, typically with pencil or paintings, sometimes delving into charcoal and Conte, I only started working digitally within the past two years. This allowed me to get more creative and create more interesting pieces. I still work with traditional mediums from time to time, but generally, those pieces are observational, the art I make digitally is much more unique to me. My main goal when creating art is to evoke a feeling, and I found I can do that with any medium if I understand it well enough, but I enjoy working in the digital sphere the most. Most commonly I like to either try and evoke an unsettling or chaotic feeling, or a very calm feeling with my work. I find when my work is presented as a collective these two feelings juxtapose to create an interesting narrative. Art has always been an important part of my life and my relationship with my work evolves and changes regularly, the pieces I make are only reflective of my current self and on represent a fragment of who I am.
