Scan

For this project I used Photoshop, Audacity, and Illustrator. I first collected images I felt reflected my topic of Nancy Burson and Facial Recognition Software. I compiled these images and created a digital composite which I then put into Audacity. In Audacity I used echo, invert, fade in, fade out, and reverb to achieve my glitch effect. Then I created a vector image in illustrator to incorporate into my image, taking inspiration from the way that facial recognition software measures your face with lines, creating an abstract topographic image of a face.
